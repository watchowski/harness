# EXCEPTIONS.md — Structured Technical Debt Log

> A rule may only be broken if the exception is logged here FIRST.
> Undated or expiry-less exceptions are BLOCKERs.

## Entry format

### E-<NNN> · <rule broken>
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Rule / principle:** e.g., "04 Disposable Blueprint"
- **Scope:** exact files/feature affected
- **Reason:** why the rule cannot be followed right now
- **Alternative safeguard:** what partially compensates
- **Expiry / remediation date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Owner:** who closes this out
